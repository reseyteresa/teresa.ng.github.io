**Course:** ECON 6018 (Master’s Level)  
**Instructor:** Teresa Ng  
**Date:** 23/01/2026  

This tutorial covers **Matrix Algebra** concepts used in graduate-level econometrics and economic theory.  
The session focuses on building intuition and technical proficiency required for subsequent topics in linear regression and matrix-based estimation methods.

---

## Topics Covered

* Matrix notation and dimensions  
* Matrix addition and multiplication  
* Identity and inverse matrices  
* Transpose and basic matrix properties  
* Economic and econometric applications of matrix algebra
